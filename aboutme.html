1.0 - About Me

My name is Mark Eames as a Chestrian, I have lived all of my life in Chester. I’m married and have two young sons, both of whom like myself are keen Liverpool FC fans. I'm fortunate enough to have a season ticket, so I get to visit Anfield regularly.

I worked for Lloyds Banking Group (LBG) for 30 years, my most recent role was a Senior Document Developer, responsible for working on the banks transactional paper & digital communications. Development was done in a tool called ‘Pitney Bowes Designer’ which is a software tool that allows the user to create a resource file that holds the conditional logic, which is then has data passed through it to create banks statements and various banking letters.

For my career progression, the most logical step was to get involved in using JavaScript, HTML, CCS and similar type languages, all of which are new to me. I came across Code Nation more by chance than anything else, I had spent some time trying to find a course that delivers exactly what this three week course does. I was very fortunate to get a spot on the course due to a last minute cancellation (fate I hope). The facilities and staff at the Chester office are both excellent and welcoming in equal degrees, and I am thoroughly enjoying the course and the content, in particular the xcode element. My aim is to secure a spot on the next 12 week course, fingers crossed.
